History
The state of Rajasthan, in India, was the site of Indus Valley Civilazition. The early medieval period saw the rise ofmy Rajput kingdoms, like Chauhans of Ajmer, Guhilot and Sisodias of Mewar in Rajasthan. Later on the region came under suzeranity of the Mughal Empire.
Cuisine
The Rajwadi Land of Rajputs is a paradise of foodies. Rajasthani cuisine is one of the most popular cuisines, boasting of a never-ending platter full of delicious dishes. It ranges from Tangy drinks to spicy starters, mouth-watering curries and crunchy breads along with added delights of papad,chutneys,pickles, and buttermilk.
